# Privacy Policy — Viet Calendar → Google Calendar

_Last updated: 23 July 2026_

Viet Calendar ("the extension") is a Chrome extension that creates Google
Calendar events from Vietnamese lunar (âm lịch) dates. This policy explains what
data it handles and what it does not do with it.

## Summary

The extension has **no server of its own**. It does not send your data to the
developer or to any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking service. The
only external service it communicates with is **Google Calendar**, using your
own Google account, to do the thing you asked it to do.

## What the extension accesses

- **Your Google account email address.** After you sign in, the extension reads
  the email address of your primary calendar so it can show which account is
  active ("Signed in as…") and let you choose which calendar to use.
- **Authentication (OAuth) token.** Signing in uses Google's `chrome.identity`
  service to obtain a temporary access token. The token is managed by Chrome and
  Google and is used only to call the Google Calendar API on your behalf. The
  extension never sees or stores your password.
- **Calendar data you create.** The event details you enter (title, date, time,
  recurrence, reminder, colour) are sent to **your own Google Calendar** to
  create, update, or delete events at your explicit request. The extension can
  also list your calendars and, if you choose, create a calendar named "Lịch Âm".

## What is stored, and where

- **On your device (local browser storage):** your settings (interface language,
  chosen calendar, default reminder and colour) and a list of events you created
  with the extension, so you can edit or delete them later.
- **Synced by Chrome:** if you use Chrome Sync, your settings may sync across your
  own devices through your Google account. This is handled by Chrome, not by the
  developer, and is not accessible to the developer.

The developer operates no database or backend and cannot access any of this data.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does **not** sell or share your data with third parties.
- It does **not** use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
- It does **not** read your browsing history, web pages, or other websites.
- It does **not** use your data for any purpose beyond its single stated
  function: creating Google Calendar events from lunar dates.
- It does **not** use your data for creditworthiness or lending purposes.

## Data sent to Google

Because the extension works with Google Calendar, the data described above is
transmitted to Google under your own account. Google's handling of that data is
governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Removing your data

- Clear the extension's stored settings and history from its **Settings** and
  **History** screens, or by removing the extension from Chrome.
- Revoke the extension's access to your Google account at any time at
  [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
- Events already created in your Google Calendar remain yours; delete them in
  Google Calendar or via the extension's History screen.
